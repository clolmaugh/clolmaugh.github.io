Deadly Austalian
SPIDERS

From the Redback spider to the Huntsman, Australian spiders are some of the most deadly in the world.

In fact, our venomous eight-legged creatures have quite the fearsome reputation across the globe. Ultimately, you’re more likely to be killed by a bee than a spider (yes, really), but that doesn’t stop the Arachnophobes among us from screaming in sheer terror when they spot a Daddy long legs in the corner.

Nevertheless, spiders are some of Australia’s most deadly species. If left untreated, their bite can prove fatal.

Family: Atracidae
Class: Arachnida
Higher classification: Atrax
Phylum: Arthropoda
Order: Spider
